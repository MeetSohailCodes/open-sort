# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus on the work.
- Respect differences of opinion.
- No harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior.

## Enforcement
Unacceptable behavior may be reported via the repository issue tracker. Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings or removal from the project.

By participating in this project, you agree to follow this code of conduct.
