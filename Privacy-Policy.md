# Privacy Policy for OpenSort

**Effective Date:** January 23, 2026

## Overview

OpenSort is a local-first media organization tool that prioritizes your privacy and data security. We understand that your personal files and data are sensitive, which is why our application is designed to keep everything on your device with no external data transmission.

## Data Collection & Usage

### What We DON'T Collect:
- No personal information is collected from users
- No files, images, videos, or documents are transmitted to external servers
- No usage analytics, telemetry, or crash reports are sent externally
- No metadata about your files is stored outside your device
- No user accounts, login credentials, or identifiers are required

### What Stays ON YOUR Device:
- All media files remain exclusively on your local system
- File organization happens entirely offline
- No cloud storage or external services are involved
- No internet connection is required for core functionality

## How OpenSort Works

OpenSort operates as a completely local application:
1. You select source and destination folders on your computer
2. The application analyzes file types, dates, and metadata locally
3. Files are organized according to your preferences within your selected directories
4. All processing occurs on your machine without network transmission

## Data Processing

- **File Types**: The application identifies file categories (photos, videos, documents, etc.) locally for organizational purposes
- **File Dates**: Creation/modification dates are read locally to organize files chronologically
- **File Names**: Names may be modified based on your renaming preferences but remain in your control
- **Metadata**: Image/video metadata is accessed only to determine creation dates (when enabled)

## Security Measures

- **Local Processing Only**: No files leave your computer
- **No Data Transmission**: Zero network traffic for file content
- **User Control**: You choose which directories to process
- **Temporary Data**: No temporary copies are stored outside your selected locations
- **Secure Storage**: All operations occur within your file system permissions

## Third-Party Components

OpenSort uses the following open-source libraries:
- Electron: For desktop application framework
- FastAPI: For local backend API
- Python standard libraries: For file operations
- All components operate within your local environment

## Children's Privacy

OpenSort does not collect or process information from children under 13 years of age. The application is designed for general audiences and operates entirely on local files that users have legal access to.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date at the top of this document. Significant changes will be noted in our release notes.
